About Me

I'm a PhD candidate in Department of Computer Science at University of Chicago. I work in SAND Lab, co-advised by Prof. Ben Y. Zhao and Prof. Heather Zheng. I received my Bachelor's degree from School of Computer science, Fudan University in June, 2017.

My research interests are generally security and privacy issues in Machine Learning especially Deep Learning. I am also interested in security and privacy of Networks and Systems such as Internet of Things (IoT).

I am a recipient of Facebook Fellowship (2020), ACM CHI Honorable Mention Award (2020), and Siebel Scholarship (class of 2021). I spent my summer with the Security + AI SIP Group @ Microsoft Research in 2020. I'm working with AI Security Team @ Meta as an intern this summer (2022).
